EE 2361 - Hollerith Project

Supported Hardware:
	- PIC24fj64ga002 microcontroller
	- Adafruit 161 Photoresistor
	- DOGS104-A I2C LCD

update_data(void)
	- Populates the structure with the most recent data from the input pins

lcd_reset(void)
	- clears LCD and sets cursor back to origin

print_data(void)
	- prints the character read by the sensors at the time of button press to LCD
